My Portfolio

Hi! My name's Anar Kansara. I've loved taking photos every since I was little girl and I still do. Photographs are powerful enough to take my breath away with their stunning composition of light, graphics, shape and color. It is an awe-inspiring moment when you see a majestically unreal photo and realize that our Earth is just that beautiful. Capturing that beauty is truly amazing and can be expressed to its maximum capacity through the art of photography.
