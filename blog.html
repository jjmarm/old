[old] A Fantastic First Club Meeting!

10/3/2011

 
Perhaps the most exciting part of a new club is the new faces and the new friendships; today was particularly pleasurable in that sense. Moreover, we began focusing on laying our goals for the year. These tend to fall in two categories:  Club Interest Ventures and Community Service Projects.

As a group, we would like to:
- teach each other and advance our personal photographic technique and style
- coordinate photo walks
- study "The Masters," including Ansel Adams and Alfred Stieglitz
- attend photography exhibitions, such as the Griffin Photography Museum
- develop a club website, to display and share our work

In terms of Community Service, we are hoping to:
- photograph dogs at New England Coonhound Rescue, as well as pursue other animal shelter opportunities
- organize and fulfill a Help-Portrait Event
- fundraise for charitable causes, by means of creating and selling greeting/post cards, as well as running a “Mystery Photo” contest
- donate portrait services to local silent auctions

Over the course of the year, we aspire to not only realize our objectives, but to go above and beyond them—it seems we will be keeping quite busy!
